Knits

Have a chunky knit or knit pillow lying around? These pieces add coziness to your space. The addition of a knit cover can upgrade any furniture, floor, or wall.

Matte Finishes

A mattle black will add modern, sophisticated element to your home. Its simplicity allows it to step back and grant other features in your room the opportunity to stand out. A piece with a mattle finish gives a nice contrast against glossy surfaces and metallic accents.

Tan/Camel

This neutral adds sophistication and warmth to your space. It's versatile shade compliments any space and just gives an overall Fall vibe.

Chic Plaid

Plaid is perfect for a variety of looks you may be going for, from vintage country to modern farmhouse. This is a versatile pattern that can be incorporated by including a plaid rug or accent pillow. A nice blanket with this pattern would work perfect for the cozy look mentioned in 'Cozy Textiles'.
