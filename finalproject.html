The Kitchen

For the kitchen, find inspiration in dark neutral colors. Use a color such as Maroon to bring in the colors of the wines and sauces of Italy. Accent with natural wood tones and metallic finishes.

The Bedroom

Where bedrooms are concerned, darker colors can create drama and romance. Dark blue and light grey can look lush and comforting in any bedroom. Brighter reds and yellows, however, can be a bit too striking in a bedroom. Opt for softer, comforting tones.

The Bathroom

For the bathroom, light blue makes an impact. When paired with cermaic or marble tiled floors, showers or countertops, it can create an aquatic feel that's immensely pleasing to the eye.

Living Room

For communal spaces like living rooms, softer tones like gray shadows, light blue, or white create a friendly and comfortable atmosphere. If you're looking for darker, warmer tones, deep ruddy browns like burgundy will make any living room seem like a great place to relax.
