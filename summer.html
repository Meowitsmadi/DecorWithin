Tropical

This season brings the tropics home. Turn any of your rooms into a light and breezy vacation space. This simple transformation can be made with the addition of large tropical leaves, wicker additions, and citrusy elements.

Splash of Color

Add color contrast with an accent piece in your space. This trend works in a variety of methods from bright pillows on your mellow couch or colorful pottery on a shelf. The eye is naturally drawn to color, your pops of color will draw attention to your beautifully created space.

Raw Finish

Add unfinished pieces in your home to add a rustic feel to your space It adds personality and fits easily into any type of room.

Mix and Match Patterns

Include lots of patterns when decorating your home this season. Mixing patterns on your furniture and decorative pieces creates a fun space full of life. It adds versatility to your room. There is a vast variety of designs that can be achieved within this look and it is best achieved on an otherwise neutral room. This way, you avoid creating a confusing space.
